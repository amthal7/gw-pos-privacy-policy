Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GW Voucher. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data, permissions, and security within the app.

GW Voucher is a merchant-focused application provided by Alamthal Co. at no cost and is intended for use as-is. The app allows authorized users to access and manage telecom voucher services through a secure internal system.

2. Information We Collect

GW Voucher does not collect, store, or share any personal data directly. The app operates within a closed merchant network and does not require user registration.

However, limited technical data may be processed to ensure the app functions securely and reliably:

  • Transaction Logs: Used internally for accounting and fraud detection.
  • Device Info: Includes IP address, device model, OS version, and usage patterns for security monitoring.
  • Error Logs: Automatically collected to help diagnose crashes and improve app performance.

This technical data is not shared externally and is used only for internal operations and diagnostics.

3. App Permissions

The app may request access to the following permissions solely for operational purposes. No data is used for advertising or profiling.

3.1 Location Access

Used temporarily for location verification and fraud prevention. Location data is not stored or shared.

3.2 Bluetooth Access

Used to connect with Bluetooth printers. No data is transmitted or tracked beyond the printing session.

3.3 Storage Access

Used to cache images for better performance. The app does not access personal media or files.

3.4 Camera Access (Optional)

Only requested when uploading a photo of a receipt for support or verification. Images are securely transmitted and not stored locally.

3.5 Notification Access

Used to deliver transaction-related updates. The app does not send promotional or advertising notifications.

4. Cookies

This app does not use cookies directly. However, integrated third-party services (such as analytics or logging tools) may use cookies to enhance performance. Users can manage cookie preferences through device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party providers to enable essential features, such as:

  • Crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Bluetooth printing support
  • Security and performance analytics

These services may collect limited technical information (e.g., device type, error data) strictly for supporting the app. They are contractually prohibited from using the data for any unrelated purpose.

6. Data Retention & Security

Only operational data required for fraud detection and accounting is retained. No personal data is stored long-term or shared for marketing.

We use industry-standard practices to secure all processed data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. External Links

The app may link to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

8. Children's Privacy

GW Voucher is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians can contact us with any concerns.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any updates will be posted here, and continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
