Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GW Voucher. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data, permissions, and security measures within the app.

GW Voucher is a merchant-focused application provided by Alamthal Co. at no cost and is intended for use as-is. The app allows authorized users to access and manage telecom voucher services through a secure internal system.

2. Information We Collect

GW Voucher does not collect, store, or share any personal data directly. The app operates within a closed merchant network and does not require user registration.

However, the app and its third-party services may process limited technical data to ensure proper functionality and security:

  • Transaction Logs: Used internally for accounting and fraud prevention.
  • Device Info: May include IP address, device name, OS version, app configuration, and usage statistics to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Error Logs: Automatically collected in the event of crashes or bugs to help us improve performance.

3. App Permissions

The app may request access to the following permissions:

3.1 Location Access

Used for security and fraud prevention. We do not store or track user location beyond verification.

3.2 Bluetooth Access

Used to connect with bluetooth printers only. No tracking or unauthorized use occurs.

3.3 Storage Access

Used to cache images for performance optimization. No access to personal media or files.

3.4 Camera Access (Optional)

Only requested when taking a receipt photo for support or verification. Images are sent securely and not stored locally.

3.5 Notification Access

Used to send important updates related to voucher transactions. No marketing notifications are sent.

3.6 Phone State (if applicable)

This may be used for security checks or to pause printing when calls are active, but is not used to collect personal data.

4. Cookies

The app itself does not use cookies. However, some third-party services integrated into the app may use cookies to enhance functionality. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party service providers to:

  • Deliver core app functionality
  • Support printing, analytics, or crash/error reporting
  • Assist in improving app performance

Examples include Google Play Services, GPS/location services, and logging tools. These services may collect limited technical data but are contractually prohibited from using it for any purpose other than supporting the app.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain only essential operational data for internal use. All data is handled in a secure environment and is never shared with third parties for marketing or analytics.

We use commercially reasonable means to protect the information processed within the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. External Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any external sites.

8. Children's Privacy

GW Voucher is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here. Continued use of the app after updates implies acceptance of the new terms.
